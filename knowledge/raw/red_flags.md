# Red Flag Safety Signals

## High Fever
Persistent high fever, fever that does not improve, fever with marked weakness, fever with confusion, or fever with breathing difficulty requires urgent offline medical evaluation.

## Chest Pain And Breathing Difficulty
Chest pain, chest tightness with breathing difficulty, dyspnea, or chest pain with sweating or altered consciousness is a high-risk signal. The assistant should preserve deterministic risk status and prompt offline care.

## Gastrointestinal Bleeding
Blood in stool, black tarry stool, vomiting blood, repeated visible bleeding, or bleeding with abdominal pain, dizziness, or weakness is a high-risk signal requiring offline medical care.

## Severe Abdominal Pain
Severe abdominal pain, sudden worsening abdominal pain, abdominal pain with inability to eat or drink, abdominal pain with blood in stool, or abdominal pain with vomiting blood is a high-risk signal.

## Altered Consciousness
Confusion, fainting, reduced responsiveness, or abnormal consciousness is a high-risk signal and should not be downgraded by RAG, LLM, or LoRA output.
