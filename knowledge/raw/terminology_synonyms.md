# Terminology Synonyms

## Digestive Terms
胃胀, 腹胀, 脘腹胀满, bloating, abdominal distension, stomach fullness, and epigastric fullness may refer to related intake descriptions. Preserve the user's wording in core state.

## Stool Terms
大便稀, 便溏, diarrhea-like stool, loose stool, constipation, dry stool, black stool, and blood in stool are stool-pattern terms used for inquiry and risk screening.

## Temperature And Cold Sensation
怕冷, 畏寒, chills, fever, persistent high fever, and fever not improving are terms that may appear in symptom descriptions. Persistent high fever is a red flag.

## Mouth And Throat
口干, 口渴, dry mouth, thirst, sore throat, and dry throat are supporting symptom terms. They do not imply a diagnosis by themselves.
